About T-pets Fashion

Our Story

T-Pets was founded in 2018 when our founder, a passionate dog lover, couldn't find fashionable yet comfortable clothing for her poodle. What started as a small Etsy shop making custom pet sweaters has grown into a premier destination for pet fashion.

From our first hand-knit dog sweater to our current collection of seasonal outfits, accessories, and luxury pet beds, we've remained committed to combining style with pet comfort and safety.

Our Mission

At T-pets, we believe pets deserve to look as fabulous as they make us feel. Our mission is to provide high-quality, comfortable, and stylish pet fashion that celebrates the unique personality of every furry friend.

We carefully design each piece with pet comfort in mind, using only non-toxic, pet-safe materials. Whether you're looking for a raincoat for walks, a cozy winter sweater, or a special occasion outfit, we've got your pet covered in style.

Our Vision

We envision a world where pet fashion is about more than just looks - where every outfit enhances the bond between pets and their owners while ensuring comfort and safety.

Our goal is to become the global leader in ethical pet fashion, setting industry standards for sustainable production while bringing joy to pets and their humans through our creative designs.

Our Values

These principles guide everything we do at Paws & Style:

  • Pet Comfort First: Fashion should never compromise comfort or safety.
  • Sustainable Style: We use eco-friendly materials and ethical production methods.
  • Inclusivity: Fashion for all pets - we design for all shapes, sizes, and breeds.
  • Community: We donate 5% of profits to animal shelters and support pet adoption initiatives.
  • Innovation: Constantly improving our designs based on pet behavior research.
